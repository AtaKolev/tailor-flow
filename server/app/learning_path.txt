Based on your career goals and personality, here is a tailored learning path for you to become a Data Engineer with a focus on Python Programming:

Step 1: Python Basics
Start with learning the basics of Python programming. As an individual who is high in conscientiousness and openness, you will likely be thorough and eager to understand the essentials. This course is highly interactive and practical, fitting well with your extroverted trait.
Course: [Python for Everybody](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/python)

Step 2: Understanding Data Structures in Python
The next step is to understand data structures, which are fundamental in Python programming. Being meticulous and organized, you'll appreciate the structured approach of this course.
Course: [Data Structures and Algorithms in Python](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/data-structures-algorithms)

Step 3: Databases and SQL for Data Science
This course is crucial for a Data Engineer. It will help you understand databases and SQL. Being high in conscientiousness, you'll appreciate the systematic and detailed approach of this course.
Course: [Databases and SQL for Data Science](https://www.coursera.org/learn/sql-data-science)

Step 4: Data Wrangling, Analysis and AB Testing with SQL
This is a more advanced course that will help you gain the necessary skills to manipulate and analyze big data. Your high openness will aid in grasping new concepts and techniques.
Course: [Data Wrangling, Analysis and AB Testing with SQL](https://www.udacity.com/course/data-wrangling-analysis-ab-testing--ud359)

Step 5: Introduction to Data Engineering
This course will give you a comprehensive introduction to the world of data engineering. It will cover the basics and also delve into more complex concepts, which should be well-suited to your high level of openness.
Course: [Introduction to Data Engineering](https://www.datacamp.com/courses/introduction-to-data-engineering)

Step 6: Advanced Data Engineering
Finally, move on to more advanced aspects of data engineering. This course is project-based, which should cater well to your medium level of extraversion, and includes complex tasks that will challenge your programming skills.
Course: [Data Engineering with Google Cloud Professional Certificate](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/gcp-data-engineering)

Remember, the pace of learning depends on your comfort and understanding. Don't rush and ensure you understand each concept before moving on to the next. Good luck on your path to becoming a Data Engineer!
