Step 1: Introduction to Python
As a foundational step, getting started with Python is crucial because it's a versatile language commonly used in data engineering. This step will help you understand the syntax and basic concepts of Python.
Course: Python for Everybody
Link: [here](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/python?)
Why this course fits: This course is designed to cater to those who appreciate detailed understanding and are not afraid of complexity. Additionally, it offers flexibility and the opportunity to dive into the subject at your own pace.

Step 2: Deepen Your Python Skills
After getting a basic understanding, it's important to deepen your knowledge in Python and understand its application in real-world scenarios to get a grasp of its practical usage.
Course: Intermediate Python Practicum
Link: [here](https://www.codecademy.com/learn/intermediate-python-3?)
Why this course fits: This course provides practical exercises which require a meticulous approach, and the content is displayed in a structured manner which allows for a thorough understanding of the topic.

Step 3: Understanding Databases and SQL
As a data engineer, you will need to understand how to work with databases. SQL is a standard language for managing data held in a relational database management system.
Course: SQL - Database Management
Link: [here](https://www.coursera.org/learn/sql-for-data-science?)
Why this course fits: This course is designed to challenge you and broaden your understanding about databases, which aligns well with those who enjoy exploring new boundaries and aren't afraid of a little bit of pressure.
